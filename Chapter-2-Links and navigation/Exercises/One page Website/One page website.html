Frozen (2013)

Frozen is a 2013 animated musical fantasy film produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios. Inspired by Hans Christian Andersen's "The Snow Queen", it tells the story of two royal sisters, Elsa and Anna, and their journey through love, fear, and sisterhood in the icy kingdom of Arendelle.

Key Information

Release Date: November 27, 2013

Genre: Animation, Adventure, Family

Running Time: 102 minutes

Production Company: Walt Disney Animation Studios

Cast & Creative Team

  • Kristen Bell as Anna
  • Idina Menzel as Elsa
  • Josh Gad as Olaf
  • Jonathan Groff as Kristoff
  • Directed by Chris Buck & Jennifer Lee
  • Music by Kristen Anderson-Lopez & Robert Lopez

Main Characters

  • Elsa: The Snow Queen with the power to control ice and snow.
  • Anna: Her fearless and loving younger sister.
  • Olaf: A cheerful snowman brought to life by Elsa's magic.
  • Kristoff: A rugged ice harvester with a loyal reindeer companion, Sven.
