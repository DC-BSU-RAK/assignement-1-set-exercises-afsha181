Plot

Frozen is a 2013 animated musical fantasy film produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios. Inspired by Hans Christian Andersen's "The Snow Queen", it tells the story of two royal sisters, Elsa and Anna, and their journey through love, fear, and sisterhood in the icy kingdom of Arendelle.
