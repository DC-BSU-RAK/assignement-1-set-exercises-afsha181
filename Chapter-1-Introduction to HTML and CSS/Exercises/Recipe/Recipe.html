Chicken Biryani

This Chicken Biryani is a flavorful Pakistani rice dish made with marinated chicken, spices,basmati rice. Perfect for special occasions or a weekend meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Water as needed

Method

  1. Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes. Then boil it until 70% cooked and drain.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, salt, and ginger-garlic paste for at least 1 hour.
  3. Heat oil/ghee in a pan. Fry the whole spices and sliced onions until golden brown.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Then add the marinated chicken and cook until chicken is done.
  5. Layer the chicken with partially cooked rice. Sprinkle chopped mint and coriander leaves.
  6. Cover and cook on low flame (dum) for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Gently mix before serving. Enjoy your biryani hot with raita!
